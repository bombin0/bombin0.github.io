Ideal Job


Business Analyst

Snapshot of Ideal Job

Description of this position:

A business analyst requires a working knowledge of both the business side and operations/ technology side of an organisation. They analyse the processes and systems within the business and assess the capability of integration with technology. Basically, they are the bridge between technology and business within the company.

This job is appealing to me because it allows me to solve business related issues with the use of technology. The salary for this job is also appealing.

Description of the skills, qualifications and experience required:

This job would require me to have strong communication skills to link product owners/specialists, operations and sales in order to produce various requirements. It requires prior experience in various roles such as; business analyst, business processes, digital projects and mobile. It also requires ‘2+ years’ of experience in the technology industry and certain skills like; read technical code and write SQL queries.

Description of the skills, qualifications and experience I currently have:

I have good communication skills and can read technical code and write SQL queries. However, I do not have any prior experience working in the technology industry.

Plan on how to obtain the skills/experience required:

My plan to reach this position involves first obtaining my Bachelors of IT and then getting a low level IT related job in which to gain experience. After working in the IT field for a while I will then apply for this job.
